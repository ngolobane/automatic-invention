Our Story

2ngTDE was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, stylish clothing at affordable prices. What started as a small online boutique has grown into a beloved fashion destination for customers around the world.

Our founder, Nqobike Kubheka, noticed a gap in the market for clothing that was both fashionable and accessible. Drawing on her background in fashion design and retail management, she created 2ngTDE to bridge that gap and make style available to everyone.

Today, we continue to be guided by our original mission, offering carefully curated collections that reflect the latest trends while maintaining our commitment to quality and affordability.

Our Values

Quality

We believe in providing products that are made to last. Every item in our collection is carefully selected for its quality and craftsmanship.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact by working with suppliers who share our values and implementing eco-friendly practices.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers and giving back to the communities that support us.

Innovation

We're always looking for new ways to improve our products and services, staying ahead of trends and embracing new technologies.
